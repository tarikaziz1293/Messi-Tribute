Career Journey

Club Timeline

2004 — 2021
🔵🔴 FC Barcelona
Debuted Oct 16, 2004 at 17. Won 35 trophies — 10 La Liga titles, 4 UEFA Champions Leagues, 7 Copa del Rey. Scored 672 goals in 778 appearances. Barcelona's all-time top scorer by massive distance. Named greatest player in Barça history.
2021 — 2023
🔴🔵 Paris Saint-Germain
Joined PSG after Barcelona's financial collapse. Won Ligue 1 in 2021-22. Formed a historic trio with Neymar & Mbappé. Used Paris to prepare for the 2022 FIFA World Cup — football's greatest individual prize.
2023 — Present
🩷🖤 Inter Miami CF
Signed July 2023. Won Leagues Cup on debut. Won MLS Supporters' Shield 2024 with record-breaking points tally. Set MLS record of 5 assists in one game. Reunited with Busquets, Alba & Suárez. Club World Cup 2025 participant — still playing at the highest level at 37.

The Maradona Echo — vs Getafe (2007)

From his first WC goal in 2006 at 18, all the way to Qatar 2022 glory. 13 World Cup goals across 5 tournaments — all in one video.
